Last week, I had the opportunity to deliver a presentation on the research conducted during the course of my MSc thesis, titled ["Exploring Unimodal, Multimodal, and Few-shot Deep Learning Methods for Diagnostic Captioning."](http://nlp.cs.aueb.gr/theses/p_kaliosis_msc_thesis.pdf) The audience comprised members of the thesis committee, fellow students, distinguished researchers, and faculty members. This thesis was conducted under the supervision of Prof. [Ion Androutsopoulos](https://www2.aueb.gr/users/ion/) and Prof. [John Pavlopoulos](https://ipavlopoulos.github.io/). The thesis committee consisted of the first supervisor, as well as Prof. [George Papaioannou](http://graphics.cs.aueb.gr/users/gepap/) and Prof. [Yannis Kotidis](https://pages.aueb.gr/users/kotidis/).

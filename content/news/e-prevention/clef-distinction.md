During the past few months, me and my colleagues from the [Mutimedia Analysis Group](https://pkaliosis.github.io/#ZgotmplZ) of NCSR "Demokritos" competed in the 2nd edition of the [e-Prevention](https://robotics.ntua.gr/icassp2024-eprevention-spgc/) Signal Processing Grand Challenge. We managed to rank 2nd outof 8 research groups on the early detection of non-psychotic relapses (Track 1) based on data from wearable devices collected from patients with psychotic disorders. We are glad to announce that we will have the chance to present our paper "A Self-Supervised Learning Approach for Detecting Non-Psychotic Relapses Using Wearable-based Digital Phenotyping" at [ICASSP 2024](https://2024.ieeeicassp.org/), which will be hosted in April in Seoul, South Korea.

I am happy to announce that our paper "A Self-Supervised Learning Approach for Detecting Non-Psychotic Relapses Using Wearable-based Digital Phenotyping" got accepted at this year's [ICASSP](https://2024.ieeeicassp.org/) Conference, which will be held in Seoul, South Korea from April 14 to April 19! Our paper focuses on a deep learning-based method for the early detection of non-psychotic relapses based on data from wearable devices collected from patients with non-psychotic disorders. This is a joint work with S. Eleftheriou, C. Nikou and T. Giannakopoulos conducted as part of our research in the [Mutimedia Analysis Group](https://pkaliosis.github.io/#ZgotmplZ) of [NCSR "Demokritos"](https://www.demokritos.gr/tender/en-0152020_1314/). We will have the chance to present our work in a few months from now in Seoul.
